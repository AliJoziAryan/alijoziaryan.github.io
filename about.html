About

As a data-driven Industrial Engineer with several years of professional experience and a strong academic background, I specialize in designing and implementing scalable data architectures that support complex business operations and enable advanced analytics. My expertise lies at the intersection of data engineering, machine learning, and operations optimization, with a passion for solving real-world problems through intelligent, scalable systems. Professionally, I have led the development of robust data pipelines using ETL tools like SSIS and Airflow, optimized complex SQL queries for performance, and modeled organizational data across diverse domains including after-sales service, quality control, production, planning, logistics, and warehousing. My work enables organizations to gain actionable insights, optimize operational processes, and make informed, data-driven decisions. I have designed analytical dashboards to track KPIs, developed machine learning and deep learning algorithms for improving planning, production, and even image processing of new products. My experience extends to market analysis, identifying export opportunities and crafting data-driven strategies for entering global markets. I've also contributed to building enterprise knowledge management systems to support predictive analytics for demand forecasting, supply chain optimization, and strategic planning. Additionally, I’ve worked on deploying data services in containerized environments using Docker and explored distributed storage solutions like Minio and Ceph. Academically, I am a postgraduate student in Industrial Engineering with research focused on optimization, supply chain, logistics, and machine learning. I have developed three versions of a metaheuristic algorithm (Multiple League Championship Algorithm – MCLA) to solve NP-hard problems in relief supply chains and have applied ML techniques to solve facility location and transportation challenges. My approach blends mathematical modeling, AI, and domain knowledge to optimize complex systems under uncertainty. With a passion for research and innovation, I am actively pursuing a PhD in Industrial Engineering, aiming to contribute to the advancement of intelligent decision-support systems in operations and logistics. I’m a self-motivated, collaborative professional eager to tackle large-scale challenges in data science, operations research, and supply chain systems.
